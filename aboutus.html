Our Mission


With our groundbreaking software, we strive to enhance the experience of interacting with the enviornment for our users and bring the future of communication right before your eyes. We believe with out technology, we are able to allow people to interact with the world in a whole new way.

Hard of hearing? Sensitive to sound? Want to be more aware of what's happening? Here at SoundScope, we have the answer.

Why use SoundScope?


We recognise that there is a huge need for more accessibility with hearing, due to the growing numbers of hearing issues. Our software has many uses and accessibility features to help with

  • Comprehending phrases when communicating with someone in loud enviornments
  • People trying to communicate with other people of different vernacular
  • Hear sounds that they may have difficulty hearing such as smoke alarms, kitchen appliance sounds
  • Traning deaf people to lipread
  • People who don't know sign language

Our Solution


Our efforts in addressing the needs for individuals with hearing related complications have led us to develop software with capabilities of displaying real-time conversations, in addition with our revolutionary audio visualization that not only interprets sounds but converts into visual cues. All of what we present here is intended to work seamlessly with AR technologies.

With built-in language translation capabilities our software takes full advantage of the fact that the user can adjust their visual output to their needs.
